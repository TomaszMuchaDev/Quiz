How to:

start the game choose region play the game register

Start of the game

To start game you need to type in your username, password and press button Login. Afterwards you will be redirected to Choose Region Page.

If you are not registered yet, just press button Register and you will be redirected to Register Page

Choosing Region

To chose region just click on image displaying part of the World you are the most interested. Image will be highlighted and a tick symbol will appear.

To confirm your choice and to start game just press button Play.

Play the game

On game page you will see three randomly picked from chosen region flags. No flag will be shown twice in one round. Your task is to chose corresponding to question flag by clicking on it. Chosen flag will be highlighted and a tick symbol will appear.

To confirm your choice and to start game just press button Submit.

After submitting your answer result will be displayed, and for good answer points will be added.

There is ten questions in one round. After completing the round you will see your total score and scores of other players. Afterwords you can play again.

Registration

In order to register all you need to do is just to fill in username and passwords fields. Your unique username will give you opportunity to match your results against other players.

We do not store or use your data.
